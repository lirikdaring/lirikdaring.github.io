4. pesan moral dari lagu history one direction


Jawaban:

The moral value of the song from One Direction entitled 'History' is that one needs to keep one's friendship as long as possible despite the difference in opinion. That will make one a great history.

Penjelasan:

To find out the moral value of the song, one has to read all part of the lyrics. Moral value is the message included in the song, either explicitly or implicitly.  It can be like the main conclusion as well.

The song 'history' by One Direction, in general, talks about friendship. Yet, implicitly, it talks about the friendship among the band's own member. The friendship in them is necessary so that they can make a history. Such is easily seen in the chorus

'You and me got a whole lot of history

we could be the greatest team that the world has ever seen

You and me got a whole lot of history

so, don't let it go, we can make some more, we can live forever.'

For them (the band), nothing is important than keeping friendship no matter what as life without friendship is nonsense. It can be seen in this verse below

'Mini bars, expensive cars, hotel rooms, and new tattoos

and the good champagne, and private planes, but they don't mean anything

because the truth is out, I realize that without you here life is just a lie'

The true friendship never breaks and find a way to keep the friends together again. That's one of the implicit message in the song. It can be seen in this line below

'All of the rumors, all of the fights

but we always find a way to make it out alive

thought we were going strong

thought we were holding on, aren't we?'

16. pesan moral dari lagu "history" one direction.


Pesan moral dari lagu "History" dari One Direction adalah agar kita selalu teguh menjalani kehidupan serta tidak terlalu mempedulikan perkataan orang lain, terlebih jika kita menjalani hidup bersama orang-orang yang kita sayangi.

Pembahasan

Pada kesempatan ini, soal meminta kita untuk menyajikan pesan moral dari lagu "History" dari One Direction.

Lagu adalah sebuah karya seni. Lagu pada dasarnya merupakan pernyataan atau karya sastra yang kemudian ditambah dengan unsur nada dan irama untuk menjadi sebuah lagu. Dengan demikian, sebenarnya yang menjadi inti dari sebuah lagu adalah isinya.

Namun, pada perkembangannya, ada beberapa jenis lagu yang kemudian beralih pada menitikberatkan pada nada dan irama yang digunakan, bukan lagi pada isi lagu tersebut.
