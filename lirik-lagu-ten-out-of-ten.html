25. A. explain about countable noun, mention min ten of them. b. explain about uncountable noun, mention min ten of them. c. explain about regular verb, mention min ten of them. d. explain about irregular verb, mention min ten of them.


Jawaban:

countable nouns are things that we can count using number

contoh: dog, cat, cup, plate, shoe, book, pencil, pen, eraser, pencil case

uncountable nouns are things we can't count

contoh: juice, milk, water, rice, money, sand, air, mud, oil, dust

regular verbs are verbs whose past tense versions are formed by adding an -ed to the end

contoh: lied, played, expected, exposed, expected, wanted, begged, climbed, cleaned, dropped

irregular verbs are verbs whose past tense versions are not formed by -ed

contoh: ran, read, went, began, said, bought, spoke, drank, sang, kept

28. tuliskan Power Of Ten dari kecil ke besar


Powers of Ten takes us on an adventure in magnitudes. Starting at a picnic by the lakeside in Chicago, this famous film transports us to the outer edges of the universe. Every ten seconds we view the starting point from ten times farther out until our own galaxy is visible only a s a speck of light among many others. Returning to Earth with breathtaking speed, we move inward- into the hand of the sleeping picnicker- with ten times more magnification every ten seconds. Our journey ends inside a proton of a carbon atom within a DNA molecule in a white blood cell.
