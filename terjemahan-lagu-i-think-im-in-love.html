6. Apa jawaban dari I think Im catching a cold


Catch a cold memiliki artian:

To become ill with the common cold.

Contoh dalam kalimat:

My sister's been sick all week, and now I think I've caught a cold from her. I hate going to the doctor's office.I always seem to catch a cold after being around all those germs!

Sehingga jawaban/respon yang tepat dari kalimat "I think I'm catching a cold" adalah:

What a pity! You should be staying at home to prevent getting worse condition.I recommend you to eat chicken soup and ginger drink. That's what I do usually when I'm catching a cold. That should help.I'm sorry. You might want to go to the doctor now, or taking medicine?
